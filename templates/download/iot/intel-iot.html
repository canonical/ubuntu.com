Real-time Ubuntu optimized for Intel SoCs

Optimized Real-time Ubuntu is now production-ready on:

  • Intel Atom® X6000E Series Processors (codename Elkhart Lake)
  • 11th Gen Intel® Core™ processors (codename Tiger Lake)
  • 12th Gen Intel® Core™ processors (codename Alder Lake S and Alder Lake N)
  • 13th Gen Intel® Core™ processors (codename Raptor Lake S and Raptor Lake P)

Coupled with Intel® Time Coordinated Computing (Intel® TCC) and Time Sensitive Networking (IEEE TSN), the real-time Ubuntu kernel delivers industrial-grade performance to the time-bound workloads of industrial enterprises.
