Install Ubuntu on AMD

Run Ubuntu on your AMD Zynq™ UltraScale+™ MPSoC-based evaluation boards, Kria™ SOMs, and Versal™ Adaptive SoC evaluation kit.

Pick the OS image to match your hardware, flash it onto SD/microSD card, load it onto your board and away you go. All images are 64-bit builds of Ubuntu.
