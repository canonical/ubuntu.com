Ubuntu Summit 25.10

October 23-24

Online

The Ubuntu Summit is a showcase for the innovative and the ambitious. We invite all experts, builders, engineers, tinkerers, designers and dreamers to join us for an unforgettable two days.

Tune in to our London livestream from the comfort of your home, from an Ubuntu release watch party, or from anywhere else around the world.

An exciting new format

{{ image(url="https://assets.ubuntu.com/v1/d6b4ccf9-pexels-pixabay-220769.jpg", alt="", width="2200", height="1650", hi_def=True, loading="lazy" ) | safe }}

This year, we’re launching a new format for the summit that aims to reflect our distributed, worldwide community.

Our hub in London will coordinate the event, but our LoCos around the world will tune in together and drive the conversation through watch parties.

With this new approach, we’re making the Summit available to a wider audience, and enabling our innovative speakers to take their impact to the next level.

Learn more ›

An event for makers. Two times over.

{{ image(url="https://assets.ubuntu.com/v1/799d61c6-6feb3ff1-community-booths.jpg", alt="", width="1200", height="800", hi_def=True, attrs={"class": "p-image-container__image"}, loading="lazy" ) | safe }}

The Ubuntu Summit always brings together some of the most inspiring people in open source. And we’re always looking to grow the impact that this has on our community.

That’s why, from this year onwards, the Ubuntu Summit will coincide with each Ubuntu release, meaning that there will be two summits per year.

This means more opportunities for the developers, engineers, designers and tinkerers from all over the world to share how they’re shaping the world through open code.

Free to attend and contribute

In true open source spirit, the Ubuntu Summit
is completely free to attend - both in person and online.

Gaming

In this topic, you'll learn about the people behind the gaming software, creative tools and and APIs that are powering this unstoppable phenomenon.

Security
