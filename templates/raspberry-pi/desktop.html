Media

The Raspberry Pi 4 brings the RAM and the graphics to watch latest films and TV shows. You'll forget you're doing it all on a single board computer.

Office suite

Create professional documents, spreadsheets and presentations to share with others with LibreOffice, the open source office suite compatible with Microsoft Office. Of course you can always use Google docs directly from your desktop too.

Develop

Ubuntu on a Raspberry Pi is the fastest way to prototype from development. Develop and test apps for ARM, plug and play with other Raspberry Pi hardware like the HQ camera and offload into the cloud.
