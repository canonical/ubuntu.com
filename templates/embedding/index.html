What's included

Our Embedding programme brings all the advantages of Ubuntu Pro together with the framework you need to deploy and distribute your products and solutions powered with Canonical offerings.

Capabilities

  • Deploy and distribute your products and solutions with Canonical offerings.
  • Direct L3 engineering support for your engineering team for the full stack from Ubuntu through MK8s to containers.
  • Certified components for compliance, hardening and audit such as FIPS 140-2, DISA STIG profiles, CIS level 1 and 2 hardening and Common Criteria.
  • 10 years security maintenance for Canonical code repositories.
  • Ubuntu systems management with Landscape and Kernel Livepatch.
  • Trademark use.
  • Joint marketing opportunities.
  • Transparent pricing model per instance and use-case.
