How does it work?

Sign-up

Fill in a 5-minute registration survey,
to help us match you with the right studies.

Recieve invites

We'll send you an invite when the appropriate study comes along.

Take part

You can decide if you want to (remotely) participate. There's usually a choice of days and times for interviews – you may also get invitations to surveys.

Talk to us!

We want to hear about your user experiences – what's working well, and what's not. Get in touch to tell us what you think, so we can improve Canonical's products and services for everyone.

FAQ

Do I need to be a Canonical customer to join?

No, you do not. Everyone is welcome!

Where can I be located?

You can be anywhere in the world. All our studies are conducted remotely.

What types of research do you do?

We do interviews, usability-tests, surveys, and more. We need your thoughts, to guide us in building better product-experiences.

What does participating look like?

Our 1:1 interview sessions are done remotely, and usually take 30-minutes to an hour. Typically youâ€™ll share your screen. Surveys usually only take a few minutes. Other tests involve completing a task with a prototype and answering some online questions.

How often will I be contacted?

Usually less than three times a year.

Will I receive something for participating?

After some lengthy moderated sessions you participate in, such as 1-1 interviews or usability tests, we'll send you a thank you gift.

What will you do with my personal information?

Our data privacy policy defines how we collect the minimum amount of personal information necessary to match you with suitable research studies.
