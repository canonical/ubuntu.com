Enterprise Business

HPE and Canonical have worked together to build a validated architecture for Openstack and Kubernetes. These validated designs include the software, hardware, and integration components for a comprehensive cloud infrastructure.

HPE and Canonical provide the support and services that the customers need to stand up production-grade OpenStack clusters, kubernetes and storage.
