Looking for Ubuntu Pro on public clouds?

Ubuntu Pro on public clouds is metered by the hour and charged through your cloud provider’s bill. Pricing is relative to your compute cost, typically between 3–4.5% of the list price. For exact rates, please refer to the pricing pages for AWS, Azure and GCP. Please note, you can also deprecate your committed spend with Ubuntu Pro.

Coverage for both open source infrastructure and applications

Ubuntu Pro covers Canonical’s complete open source portfolio, including infrastructure automation, scalable storage, private and edge clouds, container orchestration, and applications.
