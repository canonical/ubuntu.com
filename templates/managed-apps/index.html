Enterprises trust Canonical to manage their apps

  • SLA guarantee

    Assured resilience through guaranteed uptime and response to alerts.

  • App scaling

    Adapt as your business grows, we make sure your app responds to changes in demand.

  • High availability

    Lower risk and be prepared for the unexpected; your app will be fault-tolerant and ready for real-world challenges.

  • Bug fixing

    No software is perfect, and we’ll actively diagnose, debug, and then resolve bugs that appear.

  • Security patching

    Your security is our first priority. We make sure your apps stay secure and ensure no downtime with Kernel Livepatch.

  • Updates

    Stay cutting-edge, with continual support of upstream open source changes.

  • Logging

    Transparency guaranteed with full access to all applications logs through Graylog.

  • Monitoring

    Understand your app’s health at any time, using a browser, through Grafana and Prometheus based monitoring.

  • Alerts

    Proactive management with Nagios identifies and solves problems as they happen.

Service and security you can trust

Enjoy peace of mind in the security of your apps and trust the quality of Canonical's managed service - we are MSPAlliance CloudVerify certification* - which is equivalent to SOC 2 Type2, ISO 27001 / ISO 27002, and GDPR compliance.
