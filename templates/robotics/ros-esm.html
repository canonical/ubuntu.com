What is ROS ESM?

Prevent the risks of running unpatched and unmaintained ROS versions for your robot, company and customers. Risks that increase when working with End-Of-Life (EOL) ROS distributions or dependencies.

With ROS ESM, you can count on backports for critical security updates, common vulnerabilities and exposures (CVE) fixes and bug fixes for ROS and the Ubuntu base OS. Our security experts follow a standardized process to identify vulnerabilities and create and test security patches.

With ROS ESM, keeping core ROS packages secure is a fast and straightforward process.
