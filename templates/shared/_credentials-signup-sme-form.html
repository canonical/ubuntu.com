Become a subject matter expert

Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) play a critical role in the design of our professional certification exams by ensuring the exam content is accurate, relevant to current industry standards and credible.

Applicants will be selected based on professional experience and alignment with topics in development. While only those who match will be contacted, all applications are saved for future certifications.

Time commitment varies by exam development phase, SME experience, and availability, ranging from a single 2-hour meeting to biweekly 1.5-hour meetings over 8 weeks. All collaboration happens online.
