You’re a professional.
Prove it with CUBE.

The Certified Ubuntu Engineer (CUBE) is a professional endorsement indicating mastery of Ubuntu. You need to pass 15 microcerts to earn your CUBE qualification.

View the microcerts

Certification at your convenience

Earn your qualification one topic at a time, at your own pace, in any order.

The CUBE curriculum is perfectly portioned to accommodate your busy schedule. It consists of 15 separate microcerts, each covering a specific subject area, that can be taken in any order and at any time.

All CUBE badges follow Open Badge specifications.

Learn more about Open Badges

Crystallise your expertise

Prove what you know

Test your abilities through Canonical’s meticulously curated exams. Canonical-backed professional credentials are awarded for passing marks.

Upgrade your career

Onwards and upwards

Show current and prospective employers how you stand out from the rest. Individualised CUBE badges can be shared on social media, meaning that your successes will always remain visible and tied to you.

Highlight your practical skills

Real-world expertise for real-world problems

Canonical’s exams focus on useful applications of the topics at hand, ensuring that anyone with CUBE designation is well-equipped for the modern workplace. Exams occur in simulated Ubuntu terminals so that each problem looks and behaves like it would in real life.

Stay relevant

Cutting–edge credentials

CUBE certification demonstrates mastery of the latest Canonical offerings, assuring your competency in the Ubuntu ecosystem and beyond.
