Our process

Our process is easy and intuitive. You’ll initially be consulted by our specialists to design the best managed solution for your needs. Afterwards, it’s all on us:

  1. Deployment

    Specialized Canonical engineers deploy your required architecture sustainably and efficiently.

  2. Management

    Our team manages all your ‘day 2’ operations, from observability to upgrades. We handle all issues, and consistently re-enforce the security of your environment.

  3. Optional handover

    We’re lock-in free, so if after a while you want to start managing your environments by yourself, we can teach you how and give you remote support for high-severity cases.
