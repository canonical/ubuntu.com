Ubuntu Advantage for OpenStack is available in three flexible packages, to help you to scale your business in the most cost‐effective way.

Per availability zone

Availability zones in OpenStack separate compute hosts into logical groups and provide a form of physical isolation and redundancy from other availability zones, e.g., separate power supply or network equipment. To avoid having to track your individual machine count — and thereby take advantage of the elastic nature of your cloud as it scales — we offer pricing per availability zone.
