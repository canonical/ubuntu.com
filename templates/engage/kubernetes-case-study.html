Allan Gray unlocks unprecedented availability and productivity with CDK

Allan Gray, an investment banking company, originally managed their Kubernetes cluster in house, but soon realised lack of additional support and the resourcing associated with its management could pose issues.

To minimise risk and maximise revenues, Allan Gray turned to Canonical's Distrubution of Kubernetes to boost efficiency and availability.

Case study highlights

  • Allan Gray needed enterprise support for Kubernetes to ensure maximum availability for mission-critical systems
  • Canonical’s Distribution of Kubernetes (CDK) cut Kubernetes cluster deployment time from two months to two weeks
  • Canonical’s Ubuntu Advantage (UA) support saves Allan Gray two weeks of work per Kubernetes upgrade - freeing senior developers to focus on valuable DevOps work
