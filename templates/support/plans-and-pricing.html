Ubuntu offers the best economics of any commercially supported Linux solution and the best community support. Canonical offers everything you need from fully managed clouds to training and support. We aim to be as easy and economical as possible to work with and that includes flexible pricing.

Ubuntu Advantage

Ubuntu Advantage is the professional package of tooling, technology and expertise from Canonical, helping organisations around the world to manage their Ubuntu deployments. It includes access to Landscape, the systems management tool for using Ubuntu at scale, as well as 24x7 telephone and web support and the option of dedicated Canonical support engineers. Ubuntu Advantage tiers are based on the size of your deployment and the support levels you need.
