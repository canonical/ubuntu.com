Fully automated
database operations with Charmed MongoDB

The Charmed MongoDB Operator delivers automated operations management from day 0 to day 2 on the MongoDB document database. Charmed MongoDB is an enhanced, open source and fully-compatible, drop-in replacement for the MongoDB Community Edition with advanced MongoDB enterprise features.
