Cloud computing on your terms

Build your own cloud infrastructure and augment your multi-cloud strategy with a feature-rich on-prem extension. Accept no compromises. OpenStack lets you move your business to the cloud on your own terms.

{{ image(url="https://assets.ubuntu.com/v1/864680ae-cost_opt.png", alt="", width="852", height="1278", hi_def=True, loading="lazy", attrs={"class": "p-image-container__image"}) | safe}}

Cost optimization

Always run your applications where it makes most sense from an economical standpoint. OpenStack serves as a cost-effective extension to existing public cloud infrastructure, providing cost optimization capabilities and leading to a reduction in total cost of ownership (TCO) over time.

{{ image(url="https://assets.ubuntu.com/v1/f7628de4-digital_sov.png", alt="", width="852", height="1278", hi_def=True, loading="lazy", attrs={"class": "p-image-container__image"}) | safe}}

Digital sovereignty

Safeguard national interests, comply with local regulations, and regain full control of privacy. Due to the open source nature of the project and vendor-neutral governance, an OpenStack-based private cloud helps you achieve the desired level of digital sovereignty.

{{ image(url="https://assets.ubuntu.com/v1/d1c81e76-performance.png", alt="", width="852", height="1278", hi_def=True, loading="lazy", attrs={"class": "p-image-container__image"}) | safe}}

Performance acceleration

In regions of the world where hyperscalers don’t have their own data centers, high latency becomes a challenge. OpenStack enables governments, service providers, and individual organizations to build their own regional cloud infrastructure and thus meet performance requirements of cloud workloads.
